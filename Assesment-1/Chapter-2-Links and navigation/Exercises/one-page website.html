Invincible

Invincible is an animated superhero television series based on the comic by Robert Kirkman. It follows Mark Grayson, a teenager who discovers his superpowers and struggles to live up to the legacy of his father, Omni-Man.

Cast & Creative Team

Created by: Robert Kirkman

Main Voice Cast:

  • Mark Grayson / Invincible - Steven Yeun
  • Nolan Grayson / Omni-Man - J.K. Simmons
  • Debbie Grayson - Sandra Oh

Main Characters

Mark Grayson (Invincible): A teenage superhero coming to terms with his powers and responsibilities.

Nolan Grayson (Omni-Man): Mark’s father, a powerful hero with dark secrets.

Debbie Grayson: Mark’s mother, a strong and caring woman caught between her son and husband.

Reviews

Invincible has been praised for its mature storytelling, intense action, and faithfulness to the comic series.

“A brutally fresh take on the superhero genre” – The Verge
