Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Itâ€™s creamy, savory, and incredibly easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Method

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan together in a bowl.
  4. Drain the pasta, saving some pasta water.
  5. Mix the hot pasta with the egg and cheese mixture, stirring quickly.
  6. Add pancetta, mix, and season with black pepper.
  7. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan on top.
